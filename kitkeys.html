Project Overview

The main motivation for creating Kitkeys was the idea of having powerful Armenian keyboard that would motivate users to write in Armenian language. Kitkeys is an Armenian keyboard implemented for iOS devices with features that will make your typing much easier and pleasant. Functions like transliteration mode, word suggestion, word prediction, auto correction, built in emojis, multi-language support and quick cursor will make your typing faster and simpler. KitKeys has also some elements of AI and ML. Kitkeys was number 1 app in Armenian store for many months.
