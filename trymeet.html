Project Overview

Trymeet is a dating mobile application implemented for Danish market with new and revolutionary content and functions developed for this kind of service. It has booking option for different restaurants, integration with various accounting systems, chats support, flexible payments mechanism (in-app & recurring) and administration components.Two people liking each other can book a restaurant directly within the app. Also the restaurant owner has trymeet bookings module, so he will be able to not only manage the trymeet bookings but also manage the other restaurant activities. This mechanism enables us to automatically identify if there are available seats in the restaurant. Administration module was also built in the same scope to browse the statistical data, send mass emails/messages, manage bookings, make refunds etc.
